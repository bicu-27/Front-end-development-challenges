About me

I am front end developer and a Messi fan!

I've been a Messi(goat)fan for almost 10 years now. In all those years, I've experienced so many joyful moments and heartbreaking ones because of him. I supported Barcelona because of Messi and his amazing crew, and when he moved, I followed him — supporting Argentina, PSG, and now Inter Miami, all because of him. The 2014 World Cup loss was one of the hardest moments for me. I cried like a kid who lost something precious. My exercise books were filled with Messi stickers, and I couldn’t stand anyone who made him angry. To me, Messi’s enemies were my enemies. I've supported him through every moment, good or bad. And now, as I’m trying to become a front-end developer, I decided to create this website as a tribute to Messi. It’s not just a project; it’s a way to express my admiration and appreciation for everything Messi has given to football and to me as a fan. Messi is special — not just in this era, but in the entire history of football. No one else can earn my support the way Messi has.
